Green Juice 1/2 cup (4 ounces) fresh pineapple juice from about 1/3 medium pineapple, peeled; 1/2 cup (4 ounces) fresh spinach juice from about 8 ounces fresh spinach; 1/2 cup (4 ounces) fresh mango juice from about 1 large mango, peeled and pitted; 2 tablespoons (1 ounce) fresh lime juice from 1 to 2 limes, peeled Stir juices together thoroughly, divide between two glasses, and serve immediately.
